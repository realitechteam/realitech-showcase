[ FAQ ]

Questions, answered straight

How do I track my deals and commission?

Every partner gets a portal account at portal.realitech.vn: referral attribution, deal stages (new → won), commission per deal, and full payout history.

How does the partner margin work?

You quote and bill your client your own project fee. REALITECH supplies the build at partner pricing — starting 25% under list, rising to 40% with certification and deal volume — and that margin is yours, on top of whatever services you charge around it.

When do I get paid?

Commission is confirmed when a deal is marked won in the portal. Payouts run monthly by bank transfer, and every payout is recorded in your portal history.

Can I run both partner and affiliate tracks?

Yes. Some partners also keep an affiliate link for referrals outside their delivery scope. The accounts are separate and both show in the same portal.

What does it cost to join?

Nothing. No application fee, no quota, no exclusivity. Partner applications are reviewed by hand for fit — usually within a business day.

Do you compete with your partners?

No. Registered deals are protected — we don't go direct to your registered clients while you're an active partner.

What support do partners get?

Certification on RealitechEditor, co-branded sales decks, demo assets from 33+ delivered builds, and a direct line to our engineering team for pre-sales and delivery.

How fast can we start selling?

Typical path: apply this week, certify the next, co-sell within weeks — not quarters. Your first pitch can lean on our showcase and team.
