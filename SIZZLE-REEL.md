# REALITECH — Sizzle Reel Storyboard

**"Reality, Engineered." — 75-second brand sizzle**

A punchy, B2B/investor-facing montage that says one thing: *REALITECH owns the
full spatial stack — engine, twins, training, retail, mixed reality, and the next
generation.* Built to open a pitch, headline LinkedIn, or loop on a trade-show wall.

## Editing notes
- **Cut on the beat.** Every hard cut lands on a kick or snare. Pacing tightens as energy rises (2.0s → 1.5s), then the STEAM beat *breathes*.
- **Speed ramps**, not plain cuts, on the big camera moves (metro, city tour) — fast-in, ease-out.
- **Whip-pan / light-leak / glitch** transitions between *verticals*; straight cuts *within* a vertical.
- **Sound design > music.** Add a soft UI "tick/whoosh" on each label and each white flash. One riser into 1:02. One sub-hit on the logo.
- **No stock-y lower thirds.** Keep captions mono, small, cyan, with a 1px underline — same language as the site.
- **First & last 3 seconds matter most** — the claim and the logo. Spend the polish there.

## Music direction
Search a library (Artlist / Epidemic / Musicbed) for: *"cinematic technology, driving, hopeful, 120–128 BPM, build and drop, no vocals."* Reference vibe: Apple/Nvidia keynote-style — clean, confident, not aggressive EDM. Make sure there's a clear **drop ~8s in** and a **resolve ~7s before the end** for the logo.

## Deliverables to export
1. **Master 16:9** — 4K + 1080p H.264 (`realitech-sizzle-75s.mp4`)
2. **Social 9:16 cutdown (~30s)** — engine → 1 hero clip per vertical → logo. Burn captions in (autoplays muted). For LinkedIn/IG/TikTok.

4. **Silent loop version** — no logo card, for the trade-show wall / website hero.
