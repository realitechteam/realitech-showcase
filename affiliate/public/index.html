[ FAQ ]

Questions, answered straight

How do I track my referrals?

Your dashboard at portal.realitech.vn shows every referral with attribution, its stage (new → won), the commission on it, and your full payout history.

How is a referral attributed to me?

Anyone who books a demo through a link carrying your ?ref code — on any REALITECH page — is attributed to you. You can also tell us directly when you make a warm intro.

When do I get paid?

Commission is confirmed when the deal is marked won. Payouts run monthly by bank transfer and every payout is recorded in your portal history.

10% of the closed deal value, uncapped. Spatial projects are big-ticket — a single ₫300,000,000 project pays ₫30,000,000 for one introduction.

Does it cost anything?

No. Free to join, instant approval, no quota and no exclusivity — you can promote anything else alongside.

What if I can also deliver projects?

What marketing assets do I get?
